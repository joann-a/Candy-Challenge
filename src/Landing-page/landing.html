Get the important things done

In our daily lives, we found that sometimes we lack motivation to get the things that mean the most to us done. One of the main reasons for this is because no one is keeping us accountable, there is not enough pressure. This inspired us to create our application - Candy Challenge.


Candy challenge is a platform for people to interact with others by completing challenges together. With the influence of Covid-19 on the world, there has become less and less opportunities for people to socially connect .
